## Is there such a thing as a stupid question?

As a computer science student, I’ve often heard instructors say, *“There’s no such thing as a stupid question.”* I used to agree—but experience has shown me that this isn’t entirely true. Poorly thought-out questions can frustrate the person answering and may even receive unhelpful or sarcastic responses.  

The good news is that we *can* ask smarter questions, which increases the chance of receiving clear, useful answers—and also makes the experience positive for everyone involved.

---

## What makes a question “smart”?

A smart question communicates clearly, shows effort, and provides context. Stack Overflow is a great example: many programmers ask and answer questions every day, and by analyzing these, we can see what works and what doesn’t.
